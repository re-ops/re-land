Re-ops

One REPL to rule them all

About

Re-ops is a live REPL environment designed to make operations fast to react just like the real world does, its main abstractions include pipelines and operations that are easy to compose since they are plain functions. Its main components include Re-core for VM orchestration, Re-mote for remote operations and Re-gent for running distributed Clojure functions on our machines.
